Eggy Ramen

This ramen has eggs in it

Ingredients

Ramen of your choosing
egg

Directions

  1. Boil water until bubbly
  2. Insert ramen
  3. Crack egg(s) and insert
  4. Break up egg(s) with fork for 1 minute
  5. Insert soup and boil for one more minute
  6. Turn off stove and let stand for 2 minutes


Veggie Ramen

This ramen has vegetables in it

Ingredients

Ramen of your choosing
Cup of finely chopped vegetables

Directions

  1. Boil water until bubbly
  2. Insert ramen
  3. Insert finely chopped vegetables and soup and boil for one more minute
  4. Turn off stove and let stand for 2 minutes


Meaty Ramen

This ramen has meat in it

Ingredients

Ramen of your choosing
meat

Directions

  1. Boil water until bubbly
  2. Insert finely chopped meat or chicken and soup and boil for 5 minutes
  3. Insert ramen and boil for 3 more minutes
  4. Turn off stove and let stand for 2 minutes
